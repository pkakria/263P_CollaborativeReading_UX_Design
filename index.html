Team members names

Today people are reading lesser books than before, reading a book to the end requires commitment of time and attention which is difficult to give when social life is disconnected from a digital book reading experience. This leads to many unfinished books and less enthusiasm in the habit of reading. Therefore, people might not be fully engaged in reading their books.
We want to design a system which could help with this problem. The users might need an environment where they can share their thoughts on different book topics and have conversations with other book readers. Such an environment could help people stay more focused on the book they are reading by making them more enthusiastic about the reading activity.

Initial Idea

we designed a system that helps people stay engaged in reading for a longer time by allowing them to see and comment on the progress of other users. In addition, this system would allow the readers to share their thoughts on a book topic with other users to keep them engaged in reading and in conversation about books. We want to provide a social environment for book readers so that they feel enthusiastic to keep reading more with friends and are also able to see the insights from other readers on unread chapters of a book which keeps up their interest in finishing a book.
